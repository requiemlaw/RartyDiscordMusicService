# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

**Rarty. Music** ("Bot") values user privacy. This document explains what data the Bot collects, how it is used, and how your data is kept secure.

## 1. Data Collected
The Bot only processes the following anonymous data provided via the Discord API to perform its core functions (such as playing music and executing commands):
* **Guild (Server) IDs:** To know which servers the Bot is active in and to save server-specific configurations.
* **Channel IDs:** To identify the text channels where music commands are written and the voice channels where audio will be played.
* **User IDs:** To verify who executed commands and who added songs to the music queue.

## 2. Data Usage and Sharing
* Collected data is **strictly** not shared with or sold to third parties, companies, or advertising networks.
* Data is only processed temporarily to ensure the Bot's stability within the server and to manage music playlists.

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion
* The Bot does not permanently store personal data. Server settings or music queues are automatically cleared when the Bot restarts or is removed from the server.
* If you want your data to be completely deleted from our system, simply removing the Bot from your server is sufficient. For further requests, you can contact us.
